Choose Daily Questions

You must give a question that you will answer in order to record your measurements for your chosen variables every day. Be sure the question makes sense according to the variable's units and type.

Binary Example: Did you exercise today?

Continuous Example: How many miles did you run today? (1, 2.5, 3.5, etc.)

Discrete Example: How many full pushups did you do today?

Ordinal Example: What was the quality of your exercise today?
